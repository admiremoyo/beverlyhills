Welcome to Beverly Hills Group of Schools

Located at Raylands Estate, along Lower Gweru Road, Beverly Hills Group of Schools is a modern, co-educational day and boarding institution offering high-quality education from Early Childhood Development to Advanced Level.

A school built on imagination, perseverance and sustainability.

Beverly Hills Group of Schools stands as an oasis of excellence in Southern Africa. The school is bordered by the picturesque Quiet Waters Nature Reserve.

We offer a holistic, co-educational, full boarding experience, where we aim to bring out the best in our students in their academic, sporting and cultural pursuits. We are proud of the well-rounded, polite and responsible students who leave through the gates at the end of their Beverly Hills Group of Schools careers.

Our School

Junior School

Junior School (ECD – Grade 7)

Our Junior School provides a strong academic foundation for young learners. We emphasize literacy, numeracy, creativity, and moral development. Pupils learn through play, exploration, and guided instruction in a friendly and caring environment.
Subjects include English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, Shona, ICT, and Physical Education.

Senior School

Senior School (Form 1 – 6)

The Senior School offers a broad curriculum designed to prepare learners for national and international examinations. We combine traditional subjects with modern electives such as Computer Science, Business Studies, and Agriculture to ensure our students are equipped for the modern world.
Students are guided by qualified, passionate teachers who emphasize both academic and personal growth.

Boarding School

Boarding Facilities

We offer comfortable, supervised boarding facilities for both boys and girls. Our boarding life encourages independence, teamwork, and responsibility, while ensuring a family-like atmosphere. Students enjoy nutritious meals, structured study times, and weekend recreation.

OUR TEAM

We work as a team but acknowledge the individuality of team members. We embrace diversity, pro-activeness and plurality of thought in our team within a framework of collective principles of attending to students needs promptly, practically and cost effectively.

Our Career Day

Career Day 2025
At Beverly Hills Group of Schools, Career Day is one of the most exciting events of the year. Learners come dressed as their future selves — from doctors and engineers to police officers and soldiers — expressing their dreams and gaining inspiration for their life goals. The event helps students understand that every dream is valid and achievable through dedication and education.

Our Swimming Day

Swimming Day at Beverly Hills Group of Schools
Swimming Day is a favourite among our learners! It’s more than just a fun activity — it’s a valuable opportunity for students to develop water confidence, physical fitness, and team spirit. Guided by our qualified instructors, pupils learn essential swimming techniques and enjoy friendly competitions that build courage and resilience.
